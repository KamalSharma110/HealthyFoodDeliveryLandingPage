How It Works

01

Tell us what you like

Never again waste time thinking about what to eat! Veggie Patrol AI will create a 100% personalized weekly meal plan just for you. It makes sure you get all the nutrients and vitamins you need.

02

Approve your weekly meal plan

Once per week, approve the meal plan generated for you by Veggie Patrol AI. You can change ingredients, swap entire meals, or even add your own recipes.

Best chefs in town will cook your selected meal every day, and we will deliver it to your door whenever works best for you. You can change delivery schedule and address daily!
